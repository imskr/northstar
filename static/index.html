Northstar settings
Market data, portfolio, and storage
Market data
Free plan: XETR, LSE, Euronext and all European exchanges. No credit card. Without a key, Yahoo Finance delayed fallback is used.
ETF discovery is local. The Sync prices button makes a fresh request for only your selected exchange listings; ordinary screen rendering can reuse the last saved quote.
Technical diagnostics
No diagnostics recorded.
Portfolio
ETF symbols and target weights are managed from Positions → ETF library.
Storage model: your authenticated portfolio state is saved to the configured SQLite or Turso database. Browser storage is only a local working copy used during hydration and recovery. Export a backup before destructive changes.
